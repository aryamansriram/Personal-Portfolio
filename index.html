Hi, I'm Aryaman and this is my page. I am interested in working on technology for social good. Here's some info about me.

Projects

  • Hateful Memes
    Trained a combination of Resnet and BERT to classify whether memes were offensive or not. This was done as a part of Data Driven's hateful memes challenge. Achieved 0.678 best AUC score.
  • Gesture Car
    Used a hand keypoint detection model to detect gestures and used Robot Operating System (ROS) to use the gestures as signals to simulate movement of a car.
  • Flower Classifier
    Developed a custom neural net to classify different species of flowers from images. Also used transfer learning on pretrained Inception Resnet V2.
  • Melanoma Detector
    Developed a neural network to classify melanoma from images of skin lesions. Data was sourced through Kaggle.
